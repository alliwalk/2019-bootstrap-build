About

I provide thoughtful and detailed UX design and consultation for individuals, teams, clients, and projects.

What I Do

My design work centers on interaction design and information architecture. I like to think about processes and how people work.

My strengths include:

  • User research
  • Organizing complex information
  • User maps, flows, and journeys
  • Detailed functional designs

I also have excellent knowledge of modern web development and a clean design sense.

Where I've Worked

I have over 8 years of combined experience in full-time and consultant roles.

  • Worked with in-house business and agency teams, as a designer or researcher.
  • Served individual clients, non-profits and artists as a consultant.
I've worked in a lot of places.
