Email Campaign Builder

Template gallery

OverviewAttractive email templates for digital marketing campaigns.

Role: Product Designer

Tasks: Delegated tasks and supervised another designer

Keywords: Web‑based Application · Interaction Design · Information Architecture · Wireframes · Illustrator

Description: Redesign of email marketing design tools for business owners without technical or design knowledge.

Salesgenie is a subscription‑only, enterprise sales and marketing tool for small business owners. Salesgenie is a division of Infogroup. The product was released as the Email Campaign Builder.

Process / Approach

 Goal

Fix an email marketing tool that didn’t do its best for customers.

 Business Goals

The idea was to create tools that didn’t require design skill or technical knowledge.

 Design Strategy

The plans included updating the workflow and the framework of the email builder.

 Problem

Clumsy tools created customer designs that lacked nuance and hurt the Salesgenie brand.

 Exploration

Borrowed design patterns from competitors, especially if they included a mailing list.


 Design

There were 3 design phases. In the final phase, I supervised the work of another designer.

Outcome

There were other small tweaks added, such as vertical scrolling. The product was released as the Email Campaign Builder, and is currently available on the Salesgenie website.
