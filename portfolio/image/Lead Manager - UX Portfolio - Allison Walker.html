Case Study: Lead Manager

Project Overview

Helping Salesgenie customers manage the growth of their sales leads.

Description

  • Product Design role
  • Tasks included facilitating usability testing and UI design.
  • Salesgenie is a subscription-only, enterprise sales and marketing tool for small business owners.
  • The company was Infogroup.
  • Released as Salesgenie Lead Manager™.

Keywords: Web-based Application · Interaction Design · Information Architecture · Wireframes · Usability Testing · Illustrator · Photoshop

Process & Approach

Changing Goals

The project started out with the goal of supporting teams as they managed their customers.

Prior to usability testing, we planned to build out the functionality to allow teams to delegate among themselves.

Usability Testing

Usability testing revealed that teams needed a product to manage their sales leads, not customers.

This insight a changed the product strategy to focus on building a product the Salesgenie team release a product that matched user needs.


Design Work

On this project, I worked with the rest of the UX team, as well as members of product management, development, and leadership teams.

I created wireframes in Illustrator and UI elements in Photoshop. The icons in use today are the icons I selected in the wireframes.

Collaboration

This was a major project that included team members from design, product management, engineering. The VP of the Small Business division was also part of the project team, helping to guide the business case.

Outcome

The product was released as the Salesgenie Lead Manager™, and is currently available on the Salesgenie website.

New Features Include

  • Column resizing
  • Column reordering
  • Ability to add notes and tags features
  • Ability to hide or reveal lead status

In addition, a previous VoIP project also added functionality to allow users to call potential leads directly from the interface.
