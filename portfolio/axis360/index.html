Axis 360 Mobile iOS Skillshare

Project Overview

Pursuing my love of continuing education.

Role: Product Design

Tasks: Workflow, Wireframes, UI Design

Keywords: Wireframes · Mood boards · Mobile · Photoshop

Axis 360 is an e-book and audiobook mobile app that allows readers to download books for free by connecting them to their public library system. This activity was part of a suite of product design classes on Skillshare.

Process / Approach

Goal

The goal of the project was to redesign a portion of an iOS app, working through the process from sketches to design.

Plan

I began my mapping the overall tasks and workflow. I followed that by sketching interaction ideas.

Implement

I finished by creating wireframes, then mood boards, and a UI.

Work Products

Screenshots

I first took screenshots of the current app.


Task List

I created a short list of activities users can complete on the app. I focused on the act of listening to a book.


User Journey

I mapped out a user journey for how users select a book from their list of books and start listening to it. I completed the user journey in OmniGraffle.


Research


Sketches

Next I sketched out some ideas, for the new layout. I wanted to use a different layout for the book detail page, to hide actions that aren’t used often and use the available space a little better. I also changed the app navigation. I’ve found that when I’m listening to a book, and I navigate away from the playback screen, it can be hard to navigate between the playback screen and the book title screen. Audible solves this well.


Wireframes

I created 2 wireframe concepts for the book detail screen and for the playback screen. In one, my goal was to emphasize the playback controls and de-emphasize the book cover art.


Mood Boards

I completed 3 moodboards. The mood boards were for:

  • retiree listening to audio books to relax
  • working professional, commuting to work
  • skateboarding middle schooler who listens for fun


UI Design

Using the mood board for the retiree, I completed these in Photoshop CS5, using layer comps.


Outcome

SOUL released their new line of headphones, which are still available for purchase on their website. Their site has since been slighly redesigned, but still retains some of the same layout and design themes.
